# QA Manager

## Overview
The QA Manager leads the quality assurance team, establishes quality standards, manages testing resources, and ensures product quality across the organization.

## Key Responsibilities
- Manage and lead QA team
- Define quality standards and processes
- Develop test strategies and plans
- Allocate testing resources
- Coordinate with development and product teams
- Manage QA budgets and tools
- Drive test automation strategy
- Report quality metrics to leadership
- Recruit and develop QA talent
- Ensure compliance and standards

## Required Skills
- QA leadership and management
- Test strategy and planning
- Team building and mentoring
- Process optimization
- Budget management
- Risk management
- Communication and reporting
- Stakeholder management
- Technical expertise
- Project management

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: VP of Engineering or Director of Engineering
- Direct Reports: Senior QA Engineers, QA Engineers, Test Automation Engineers

## Success Metrics
- Product quality metrics
- Team productivity and efficiency
- Test coverage and effectiveness
- Defect escape rate
- Team satisfaction and retention
- Process improvement initiatives
- Budget adherence
- Release quality
- Customer-reported defects
