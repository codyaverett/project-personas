# Product Owner

## Overview
The Product Owner maximizes product value by managing the product backlog, defining acceptance criteria, and serving as the primary liaison between stakeholders and the development team.

## Key Responsibilities
- Manage and prioritize product backlog
- Write detailed user stories and acceptance criteria
- Collaborate closely with development team
- Participate in sprint planning and reviews
- Make rapid decisions on requirements
- Communicate with stakeholders
- Validate completed work
- Manage scope and timeline expectations
- Gather and incorporate feedback
- Ensure delivery of value

## Required Skills
- Agile/Scrum expertise
- Requirements definition
- Stakeholder communication
- Prioritization skills
- Decision-making
- Detail-oriented
- Team collaboration
- Problem-solving
- Time management
- Domain knowledge

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: Product Manager or Product Director
- Direct Reports: None

## Success Metrics
- Sprint goal achievement
- Backlog health and readiness
- Story acceptance rate
- Velocity trends
- Stakeholder satisfaction
- Requirements clarity
- Team collaboration effectiveness
- Value delivered per sprint
- Defect rates
