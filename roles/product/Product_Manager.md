# Product Manager

## Overview
The Product Manager owns the product vision for their area, defines requirements, prioritizes features, and works cross-functionally to deliver products that solve customer problems.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define product requirements and user stories
- Manage product backlog and prioritization
- Conduct user research and gather feedback
- Analyze market trends and competition
- Collaborate with engineering and design
- Define success metrics and KPIs
- Create product roadmaps
- Manage stakeholder expectations
- Drive go-to-market strategies
- Monitor product performance

## Required Skills
- Product management methodologies
- User research and analysis
- Data analysis and metrics
- Communication and presentation
- Stakeholder management
- Prioritization and decision-making
- Market analysis
- Agile/Scrum methodologies
- Business acumen
- Problem-solving

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: VP of Product or Product Director
- Direct Reports: None (may lead Associate PMs)

## Success Metrics
- Product adoption and usage
- Feature success rate
- Customer satisfaction
- Revenue impact
- Time to market
- User engagement metrics
- Product quality
- Stakeholder satisfaction
- Market share growth
