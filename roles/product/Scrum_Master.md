# Scrum Master

## Overview
The Scrum Master facilitates agile processes, removes impediments, coaches teams in Scrum practices, and ensures teams can deliver value efficiently.

## Key Responsibilities
- Facilitate Scrum ceremonies
- Remove team impediments
- Coach team on agile practices
- Shield team from distractions
- Track team metrics and velocity
- Foster team collaboration
- Manage stakeholder expectations
- Promote continuous improvement
- Ensure process compliance
- Mediate conflicts

## Required Skills
- Scrum/Agile expertise
- Facilitation skills
- Coaching and mentoring
- Conflict resolution
- Communication
- Servant leadership
- Problem-solving
- Organizational skills
- Metrics and reporting
- Change management

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: Engineering Manager or Agile Coach
- Direct Reports: None (servant leader)

## Success Metrics
- Team velocity stability
- Sprint goal achievement
- Impediment resolution time
- Team satisfaction
- Process adherence
- Retrospective action items
- Stakeholder satisfaction
- Team collaboration index
- Continuous improvement metrics
