# Senior Product Manager

## Overview
The Senior Product Manager leads complex product initiatives, mentors other PMs, and drives strategic product decisions while maintaining deep customer and market understanding.

## Key Responsibilities
- Own product strategy for major product areas
- Lead cross-functional product initiatives
- Mentor junior product managers
- Drive product vision and roadmap
- Conduct advanced market analysis
- Build business cases for new products
- Manage complex stakeholder relationships
- Lead pricing and packaging decisions
- Define product metrics and success criteria
- Coordinate product launches

## Required Skills
- Advanced product management
- Strategic thinking and planning
- Leadership and mentoring
- Data-driven decision making
- Customer empathy
- Business strategy
- Communication and influence
- Market research
- Financial modeling
- Cross-functional leadership

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: Product Director or VP of Product
- Direct Reports: Product Managers, Associate PMs

## Success Metrics
- Product line revenue
- Strategic initiative success
- Team development
- Customer retention
- Product innovation
- Market penetration
- Profitability metrics
- Launch success rates
- Competitive positioning
