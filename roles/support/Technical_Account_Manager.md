# Technical Account Manager

## Overview
The Technical Account Manager serves as the primary technical liaison for strategic accounts, ensuring technical success and driving value through deep product expertise.

## Key Responsibilities
- Provide technical guidance to key accounts
- Lead technical implementations
- Conduct architecture reviews
- Develop technical success plans
- Troubleshoot complex issues
- Deliver technical training
- Advocate for customer needs
- Manage technical escalations
- Drive product adoption
- Support pre-sales activities

## Required Skills
- Deep technical expertise
- Solution architecture
- Customer relationship management
- Communication skills
- Project management
- Problem-solving
- Presentation skills
- Business understanding
- Product knowledge
- Consultative approach

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: Head of Customer Success or VP of Customer Success
- Direct Reports: None

## Success Metrics
- Account health and satisfaction
- Technical implementation success
- Issue resolution effectiveness
- Product adoption depth
- Account retention
- Technical training delivered
- Escalation prevention
- Customer advocacy
- Revenue retention
