# Customer Support Engineer

## Overview
The Customer Support Engineer provides technical assistance to customers, troubleshoots issues, and ensures customer satisfaction through effective problem resolution.

## Key Responsibilities
- Respond to customer support tickets
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Provide product guidance and training
- Document solutions and FAQs
- Escalate complex issues
- Collaborate with engineering on bugs
- Monitor customer satisfaction
- Contribute to knowledge base
- Participate in on-call rotation
- Gather customer feedback

## Required Skills
- Technical troubleshooting
- Customer service excellence
- Communication skills
- Product knowledge
- Problem-solving
- Patience and empathy
- Documentation skills
- Basic scripting/coding
- Time management
- Ticket management systems

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: Support Manager
- Direct Reports: None

## Success Metrics
- Ticket resolution time
- Customer satisfaction score
- First contact resolution rate
- Ticket volume handled
- Knowledge base contributions
- Escalation rate
- Response time SLA
- Customer retention impact
- Technical accuracy
