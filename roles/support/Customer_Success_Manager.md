# Customer Success Manager

## Overview
The Customer Success Manager ensures customers achieve their desired outcomes, drives product adoption, manages relationships, and maximizes customer lifetime value.

## Key Responsibilities
- Manage customer relationships and accounts
- Drive product adoption and usage
- Conduct customer onboarding
- Identify expansion opportunities
- Monitor customer health metrics
- Prevent churn and drive renewals
- Gather product feedback
- Conduct business reviews
- Coordinate with sales and support
- Develop success plans

## Required Skills
- Relationship management
- Strategic thinking
- Communication and presentation
- Data analysis
- Project management
- Product expertise
- Business acumen
- Problem-solving
- Negotiation
- Empathy

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: Head of Customer Success or VP of Customer Success
- Direct Reports: None

## Success Metrics
- Customer retention rate
- Net promoter score (NPS)
- Product adoption metrics
- Expansion revenue
- Customer health scores
- Renewal rate
- Time to value
- Customer satisfaction
- Upsell/cross-sell success
