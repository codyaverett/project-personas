# Head of Customer Success

## Overview
The Head of Customer Success leads the customer success organization, develops strategies to maximize customer value, retention, and growth while building a customer-centric culture.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define customer success strategy
- Lead customer success teams
- Establish success metrics and KPIs
- Drive customer retention and growth
- Develop customer engagement programs
- Oversee onboarding and adoption
- Manage customer health monitoring
- Build executive relationships
- Collaborate with product and sales
- Report on customer metrics

## Required Skills
- Customer success leadership
- Strategic planning
- Team management
- Data-driven decision making
- Stakeholder management
- Process optimization
- Financial acumen
- Communication skills
- Change management
- Customer empathy

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: COO or CEO
- Direct Reports: Customer Success Managers, Technical Account Managers, Support Manager

## Success Metrics
- Gross retention rate
- Net retention rate
- Customer lifetime value
- NPS and CSAT scores
- Product adoption rates
- Time to value
- Expansion revenue
- Team productivity
- Customer health scores
