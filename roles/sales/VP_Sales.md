# Vice President of Sales

## Overview
The VP of Sales leads the sales organization, develops sales strategies, manages sales teams, and drives revenue growth through new customer acquisition and expansion.

## Key Responsibilities
- Build and lead high-performing sales teams
- Develop sales strategies and playbooks
- Set and achieve sales quotas
- Manage sales pipeline and forecasting
- Optimize sales processes and methodologies
- Coach and develop sales talent
- Build relationships with key accounts
- Collaborate with marketing and product
- Negotiate enterprise deals
- Analyze sales metrics and performance

## Required Skills
- Sales leadership and management
- Strategic thinking and planning
- Pipeline management
- Team building and coaching
- Data analysis and forecasting
- Negotiation skills
- CRM expertise
- Communication and presentation
- Customer relationship management
- Business development

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: CRO or CEO
- Direct Reports: Sales Directors, Sales Managers, Enterprise Account Executives

## Success Metrics
- Sales quota attainment
- Pipeline generation and coverage
- Average deal size
- Sales cycle length
- Win rate
- Team productivity metrics
- Customer acquisition numbers
- Territory performance
- Forecast accuracy
