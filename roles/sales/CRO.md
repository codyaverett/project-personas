# Chief Revenue Officer (CRO)

## Overview
The CRO oversees all revenue-generating processes, aligning sales, marketing, and customer success to drive sustainable revenue growth and achieve business targets.

## Key Responsibilities
- Develop and execute revenue strategy
- Align sales, marketing, and customer success teams
- Set and achieve revenue targets
- Optimize the entire revenue funnel
- Lead pricing and packaging strategies
- Build strategic partnerships
- Manage revenue forecasting and planning
- Drive customer acquisition and retention
- Report revenue metrics to board
- Scale go-to-market operations

## Required Skills
- Revenue operations expertise
- Sales and marketing leadership
- Strategic planning
- Data-driven decision making
- Team building and management
- Financial acumen
- Customer lifecycle understanding
- Negotiation and deal-making
- Market analysis
- Executive communication

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: CEO
- Direct Reports: VP of Sales, VP of Marketing, VP of Customer Success

## Success Metrics
- Annual recurring revenue (ARR) growth
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC) to LTV ratio
- Sales pipeline health
- Win rate and deal velocity
- Net revenue retention
- Market share growth
- Sales productivity
- Revenue predictability
- Customer expansion revenue
