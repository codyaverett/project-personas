# Sales Engineer / Solutions Architect

## Overview
The Sales Engineer provides technical expertise during the sales process, demonstrating product capabilities, designing solutions, and ensuring technical fit for customer requirements.

## Key Responsibilities
- Deliver technical product demonstrations
- Design customer-specific solutions
- Conduct proof of concepts (POCs)
- Answer technical questions during sales
- Assess technical requirements and feasibility
- Create technical proposals and documentation
- Support RFP/RFI responses
- Collaborate with product and engineering
- Train customers on technical aspects
- Provide pre-sales technical support

## Required Skills
- Deep technical product knowledge
- Solution architecture skills
- Presentation and communication
- Problem-solving abilities
- Customer-facing skills
- Technical writing
- Project management
- Industry knowledge
- Consultative approach
- Programming/scripting basics

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: Sales Engineering Manager or VP of Sales
- Direct Reports: None

## Success Metrics
- POC success rate
- Technical win rate
- Demo effectiveness
- Customer technical satisfaction
- Sales cycle acceleration
- Solution quality scores
- Revenue influenced
- Technical objection handling
- Knowledge transfer effectiveness
