# Sales Development Representative (SDR)

## Overview
The SDR focuses on outbound prospecting, qualifying leads, and booking meetings for Account Executives, serving as the first point of contact for potential customers.

## Key Responsibilities
- Conduct outbound prospecting via calls, emails, and social
- Qualify inbound leads
- Book qualified meetings for Account Executives
- Research target accounts and contacts
- Maintain accurate data in CRM
- Follow up on marketing campaigns
- Achieve activity and meeting quotas
- Collaborate with marketing on campaigns
- Develop initial relationships with prospects
- Identify key decision makers

## Required Skills
- Prospecting and cold outreach
- Communication skills (written and verbal)
- Active listening
- Time management
- Persistence and resilience
- CRM and sales tools proficiency
- Research skills
- Social selling
- Basic product knowledge
- Goal orientation

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: SDR Manager or Sales Manager
- Direct Reports: None

## Success Metrics
- Qualified meetings booked
- Activity metrics (calls, emails, connects)
- Conversion rates
- Pipeline generated
- Lead response time
- Data quality in CRM
- Quota attainment
- Meeting show rate
- Prospect engagement rate
