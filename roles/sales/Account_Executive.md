# Account Executive

## Overview
The Account Executive manages the full sales cycle from prospecting to closing, building relationships with prospects and customers to drive new business revenue.

## Key Responsibilities
- Manage full sales cycle from lead to close
- Qualify prospects and identify opportunities
- Conduct product demonstrations
- Develop and present proposals
- Negotiate contracts and pricing
- Build and maintain customer relationships
- Meet or exceed sales quotas
- Maintain accurate CRM records
- Collaborate with customer success on handoffs
- Identify upsell and cross-sell opportunities

## Required Skills
- Sales methodology expertise
- Prospecting and lead qualification
- Presentation and demo skills
- Negotiation and closing
- Relationship building
- Time management
- CRM proficiency
- Business acumen
- Communication skills
- Problem-solving

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: Sales Manager or Sales Director
- Direct Reports: None

## Success Metrics
- Quota attainment
- Number of deals closed
- Average deal size
- Sales cycle time
- Pipeline generation
- Activity metrics (calls, meetings, demos)
- Win rate
- Customer satisfaction
- Forecast accuracy
