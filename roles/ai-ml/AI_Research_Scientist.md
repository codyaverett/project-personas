# AI Research Scientist

## Overview
The AI Research Scientist conducts cutting-edge research in artificial intelligence, developing new algorithms, publishing papers, and advancing the state of the art in AI/ML.

## Key Responsibilities
- Conduct original AI/ML research
- Develop novel algorithms and approaches
- Publish research papers and patents
- Present at conferences
- Collaborate with academic institutions
- Prototype experimental systems
- Review latest research literature
- Mentor junior researchers
- Transfer research to product teams
- Contribute to open source

## Required Skills
- Deep learning and neural networks
- Mathematical foundations (linear algebra, statistics)
- Research methodology
- Scientific writing
- Programming (Python, C++)
- ML frameworks expertise
- Problem formulation
- Experimental design
- Critical thinking
- Communication skills

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: Head of Research or Chief Scientist
- Direct Reports: Research Engineers, PhD Students

## Success Metrics
- Publication impact and citations
- Patent applications
- Conference presentations
- Research-to-product transfer
- Innovation metrics
- Collaboration effectiveness
- Grant funding secured
- Open source contributions
- Thought leadership
