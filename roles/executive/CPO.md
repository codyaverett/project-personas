# Chief Product Officer (CPO)

## Overview
The CPO leads the product management and development strategy, ensuring products meet market needs and drive business growth while maintaining alignment with company vision.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define product vision and strategy
- Lead product roadmap development and prioritization
- Oversee product lifecycle management
- Drive market research and competitive analysis
- Collaborate with engineering on product development
- Ensure product-market fit
- Manage product portfolio optimization
- Lead pricing and positioning strategies
- Drive product innovation and differentiation
- Oversee user experience and design standards

## Required Skills
- Product management expertise
- Strategic thinking and vision
- Market analysis and customer insights
- Data analysis and metrics-driven decision making
- Leadership and cross-functional collaboration
- User experience and design thinking
- Agile and lean methodologies
- Business acumen and commercial awareness
- Communication and stakeholder management
- Innovation and creativity

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: CEO
- Direct Reports: VP of Product, Product Directors, Head of Design, Product Managers

## Success Metrics
- Product revenue and growth
- User adoption and engagement
- Customer satisfaction and NPS
- Time to market
- Product quality metrics
- Feature adoption rates
- Market share
- Product profitability
- Innovation pipeline health
