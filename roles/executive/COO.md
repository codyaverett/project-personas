# Chief Operating Officer (COO)

## Overview
The COO oversees the day-to-day administrative and operational functions of a business, translating the CEO's vision into operational execution and ensuring efficient business operations.

## Key Responsibilities
- Manage daily operations across all departments
- Implement business strategies and plans
- Optimize operational processes and workflows
- Oversee supply chain and logistics
- Manage vendor and partner relationships
- Drive operational excellence and efficiency
- Lead cross-functional initiatives and projects
- Establish and monitor KPIs across departments
- Ensure quality control and customer satisfaction
- Manage operational budgets and resource allocation

## Required Skills
- Operational management expertise
- Process improvement and optimization
- Strategic planning and execution
- Leadership and team management
- Data-driven decision making
- Change management
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Problem-solving and crisis management
- Financial acumen
- Communication and negotiation

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: CEO
- Direct Reports: VP of Operations, VP of Business Operations, Department Heads

## Success Metrics
- Operational efficiency metrics
- Cost per unit/transaction
- Process improvement initiatives
- Customer satisfaction scores
- On-time delivery rates
- Quality metrics
- Employee productivity
- Revenue per employee
- Operational budget adherence
