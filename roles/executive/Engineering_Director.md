# Engineering Director

## Overview
The Engineering Director manages multiple engineering teams, provides technical leadership, and ensures alignment between engineering execution and business objectives.

## Key Responsibilities
- Manage multiple engineering teams and managers
- Drive technical strategy for assigned area
- Ensure project delivery and quality standards
- Collaborate with product and design teams
- Mentor and develop engineering talent
- Oversee technical architecture decisions
- Manage team budgets and resources
- Drive process improvements
- Handle escalations and technical decisions
- Foster team culture and morale

## Required Skills
- Technical leadership and expertise
- People management and development
- Project and program management
- Strategic thinking and planning
- Communication and collaboration
- Problem-solving and decision-making
- Agile methodologies
- Technical architecture
- Conflict resolution
- Budget management

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: VP of Engineering
- Direct Reports: Engineering Managers, Senior Engineers, Tech Leads

## Success Metrics
- Team delivery and velocity
- Product quality metrics
- Team satisfaction and retention
- Project success rates
- Technical debt reduction
- Code review efficiency
- Incident response times
- Team growth and development
- Cross-team collaboration
