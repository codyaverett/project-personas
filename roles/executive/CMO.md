# Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)

## Overview
The CMO oversees all marketing activities, brand management, and customer acquisition strategies to drive business growth and market presence.

## Key Responsibilities
- Develop and execute marketing strategy
- Oversee brand management and positioning
- Lead demand generation and customer acquisition
- Manage marketing budgets and ROI
- Drive digital marketing and online presence
- Oversee content strategy and marketing communications
- Lead market research and customer insights
- Manage public relations and corporate communications
- Develop partnership and channel strategies
- Align marketing with sales and product teams

## Required Skills
- Marketing strategy and execution
- Brand management
- Digital marketing expertise
- Data analytics and marketing metrics
- Creative direction
- Leadership and team management
- Budget management
- Communication and presentation
- Customer psychology and behavior
- Technology and marketing tools

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: CEO
- Direct Reports: VP of Marketing, VP of Growth, Head of Brand, Head of Demand Generation

## Success Metrics
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Marketing qualified leads (MQLs)
- Brand awareness and perception
- Marketing ROI and attribution
- Website traffic and conversion rates
- Content engagement metrics
- Campaign performance
- Customer lifetime value
- Market share growth
