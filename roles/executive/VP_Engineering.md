# Vice President of Engineering

## Overview
The VP of Engineering leads the engineering organization, manages engineering teams, and ensures successful delivery of technical products while maintaining high engineering standards.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead and scale engineering teams
- Drive engineering strategy and technical roadmap
- Ensure on-time delivery of quality products
- Manage engineering budgets and resources
- Foster engineering culture and best practices
- Oversee technical architecture decisions
- Collaborate with product on feature development
- Implement agile development processes
- Manage technical debt and system reliability
- Recruit and develop engineering talent

## Required Skills
- Engineering leadership and management
- Technical expertise and architecture
- Agile and software development methodologies
- Team building and mentoring
- Project and program management
- Strategic planning
- Communication and collaboration
- Problem-solving and decision-making
- Budget management
- Stakeholder management

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: CTO
- Direct Reports: Engineering Directors, Engineering Managers, Principal Engineers

## Success Metrics
- Product delivery timelines
- Engineering velocity and productivity
- Code quality metrics
- System reliability and uptime
- Team satisfaction and retention
- Technical debt management
- Sprint completion rates
- Bug resolution time
- Engineering cost efficiency
