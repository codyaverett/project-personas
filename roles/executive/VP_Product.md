# Vice President of Product

## Overview
The VP of Product drives product strategy and execution, leading product management teams to deliver products that meet customer needs and business objectives.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define product strategy and vision
- Lead product roadmap planning
- Manage product managers and teams
- Drive product discovery and validation
- Oversee product launches and go-to-market
- Collaborate with engineering and design
- Analyze market trends and competition
- Manage product metrics and analytics
- Ensure product-market fit
- Drive product innovation

## Required Skills
- Product management expertise
- Strategic thinking and planning
- Customer research and insights
- Data analysis and metrics
- Leadership and team management
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Market analysis
- Communication and presentation
- Business acumen
- Agile methodologies

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: CPO or CEO
- Direct Reports: Product Directors, Senior Product Managers, Product Managers

## Success Metrics
- Product adoption and usage
- Revenue growth
- Customer satisfaction
- Feature success rates
- Time to market
- Product quality metrics
- User engagement
- Market share
- Product profitability
