# General Counsel

## Overview
The General Counsel serves as the chief legal officer, providing strategic legal advice, managing legal risks, ensuring compliance, and protecting the company's interests.

## Key Responsibilities
- Provide strategic legal counsel to executives
- Manage legal risks and compliance
- Oversee contracts and negotiations
- Handle corporate governance matters
- Manage litigation and disputes
- Ensure regulatory compliance
- Lead M&A legal activities
- Manage intellectual property portfolio
- Oversee data privacy and security laws
- Build and manage legal team

## Required Skills
- Legal expertise and bar admission
- Business acumen
- Risk management
- Contract negotiation
- Regulatory knowledge
- Strategic thinking
- Leadership and management
- Communication skills
- Problem-solving
- Ethics and integrity

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: CEO
- Direct Reports: Legal team members, Compliance Manager

## Success Metrics
- Legal risk mitigation
- Compliance audit results
- Contract turnaround time
- Litigation success rate
- Legal spend efficiency
- Regulatory compliance score
- IP portfolio value
- Board satisfaction
- Legal team effectiveness
