# Privacy Officer / Data Protection Officer (DPO)

## Overview
The Privacy Officer ensures organizational compliance with data protection laws, manages privacy programs, and safeguards personal data handling practices.

## Key Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain privacy policies
- Ensure GDPR, CCPA, and other compliance
- Conduct privacy impact assessments
- Manage data subject requests
- Train employees on privacy practices
- Handle privacy incidents and breaches
- Liaise with regulatory authorities
- Monitor data processing activities
- Maintain records of processing
- Advise on privacy by design

## Required Skills
- Privacy law expertise
- Regulatory compliance
- Risk assessment
- Policy development
- Training and communication
- Incident management
- Audit and monitoring
- Stakeholder management
- Technical understanding
- Attention to detail

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: General Counsel or CISO
- Direct Reports: Privacy Analysts

## Success Metrics
- Compliance audit scores
- Privacy incident rate
- Training completion rates
- Data subject request response time
- Privacy assessment coverage
- Regulatory fine avoidance
- Policy adherence rates
- Breach notification timeliness
- Privacy program maturity
