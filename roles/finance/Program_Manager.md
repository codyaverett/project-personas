# Program Manager

## Overview
The Program Manager oversees multiple related projects, ensuring strategic alignment, managing dependencies, and delivering complex initiatives that span teams and departments.

## Key Responsibilities
- Manage portfolio of related projects
- Ensure strategic alignment
- Coordinate cross-project dependencies
- Manage program budget and resources
- Report to executive stakeholders
- Identify and manage program risks
- Drive organizational change
- Establish program governance
- Measure program benefits
- Lead program team

## Required Skills
- Program management expertise
- Strategic thinking
- Leadership and influence
- Complex project coordination
- Stakeholder management
- Risk management
- Budget management
- Change management
- Communication skills
- Business acumen

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: VP of Operations or COO
- Direct Reports: Project Managers

## Success Metrics
- Program delivery success
- Strategic objective achievement
- Budget performance
- Benefit realization
- Stakeholder satisfaction
- Risk mitigation
- Resource optimization
- Cross-team collaboration
- Change adoption rate
