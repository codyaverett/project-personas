# Business Analyst

## Overview
The Business Analyst bridges business and technology, analyzing processes, gathering requirements, and recommending solutions to improve business operations and outcomes.

## Key Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements
- Analyze business processes and workflows
- Identify improvement opportunities
- Create functional specifications
- Facilitate stakeholder meetings
- Perform data analysis
- Support solution design and testing
- Develop business cases
- Create process documentation
- Manage change requests

## Required Skills
- Business analysis techniques
- Requirements gathering
- Process modeling
- Data analysis
- SQL and data tools
- Documentation skills
- Communication and facilitation
- Problem-solving
- Critical thinking
- Domain knowledge

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: Product Manager or Business Operations Manager
- Direct Reports: None

## Success Metrics
- Requirements quality and completeness
- Process improvement impact
- Stakeholder satisfaction
- Project success rate
- Analysis turnaround time
- Cost savings identified
- Documentation quality
- Change request accuracy
- Business value delivered
