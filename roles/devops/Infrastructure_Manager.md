# Infrastructure Manager

## Overview
The Infrastructure Manager leads the infrastructure team, manages IT operations, ensures system reliability, and aligns infrastructure strategy with business goals.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead infrastructure and operations teams
- Define infrastructure strategy
- Manage infrastructure budgets
- Ensure system availability and performance
- Oversee disaster recovery planning
- Manage vendor relationships
- Drive infrastructure modernization
- Implement security measures
- Coordinate with engineering teams
- Report metrics to leadership

## Required Skills
- Infrastructure management
- Team leadership
- Budget management
- Strategic planning
- Cloud and on-premise systems
- Security and compliance
- Vendor management
- Project management
- Communication
- Risk management

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: CTO or VP of Engineering
- Direct Reports: DevOps Engineers, SREs, System Administrators

## Success Metrics
- System uptime and availability
- Infrastructure costs
- Team productivity
- Incident resolution times
- Project delivery success
- Security compliance
- Modernization progress
- Vendor performance
- Budget adherence
