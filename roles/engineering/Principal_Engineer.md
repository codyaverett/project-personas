# Principal Engineer

## Overview
The Principal Engineer provides technical leadership across multiple teams, drives architectural decisions, and sets technical direction for major initiatives and systems.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define technical strategy and architecture
- Lead cross-team technical initiatives
- Drive technical standards and best practices
- Mentor senior engineers and technical leaders
- Solve complex technical challenges
- Evaluate and introduce new technologies
- Represent engineering in strategic decisions
- Lead technical reviews and design sessions
- Influence product and business strategy
- Build consensus on technical direction

## Required Skills
- Deep technical expertise
- System architecture and design
- Technical leadership
- Strategic thinking
- Communication and influence
- Cross-team collaboration
- Problem-solving at scale
- Technology evaluation
- Mentoring and coaching
- Business acumen

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: CTO or VP of Engineering
- Direct Reports: None (influences across teams)

## Success Metrics
- Architectural improvements
- Technical initiative success
- Cross-team impact
- Technical debt reduction
- Innovation and patents
- Mentorship effectiveness
- Technology adoption
- System performance improvements
- Technical decision quality
