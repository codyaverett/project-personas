# Engineering Manager

## Overview
The Engineering Manager leads a team of engineers, balances technical and people management responsibilities, and ensures successful project delivery while developing team members.

## Key Responsibilities
- Manage and mentor a team of engineers
- Plan and prioritize team workload
- Conduct performance reviews and career development
- Facilitate agile ceremonies and processes
- Remove blockers and enable team productivity
- Collaborate with product managers on requirements
- Ensure code quality and technical standards
- Manage team hiring and onboarding
- Foster team culture and collaboration
- Handle technical escalations

## Required Skills
- People management and leadership
- Technical expertise
- Agile methodologies
- Communication and collaboration
- Conflict resolution
- Project management
- Mentoring and coaching
- Problem-solving
- Time management
- Emotional intelligence

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: Engineering Director or VP of Engineering
- Direct Reports: Software Engineers, Senior Engineers

## Success Metrics
- Team velocity and delivery
- Team satisfaction and engagement
- Code quality metrics
- On-time delivery
- Team retention
- Individual growth plans
- Sprint completion rates
- Bug rates and resolution time
- Team collaboration effectiveness
