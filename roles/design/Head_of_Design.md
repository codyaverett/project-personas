# Head of Design

## Overview
The Head of Design leads the design organization, sets design vision and strategy, and ensures design excellence across all products and customer touchpoints.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define design vision and strategy
- Lead and manage design teams
- Establish design processes and standards
- Drive design system development
- Collaborate with product and engineering leadership
- Champion user-centered design
- Manage design budgets and resources
- Recruit and develop design talent
- Present design strategy to executives
- Foster design culture

## Required Skills
- Design leadership
- Strategic thinking
- Team management
- Design expertise
- Business acumen
- Communication and presentation
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Change management
- Budget management
- Innovation

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: CPO or CEO
- Direct Reports: UX Designers, UI Designers, Design Managers

## Success Metrics
- Design team productivity
- Product usability scores
- Design system adoption
- Team satisfaction and retention
- Design innovation
- Time to market
- Customer satisfaction
- Design ROI
- Brand perception
