# Data Analyst

## Overview
The Data Analyst collects, processes, and performs statistical analyses on data to help organizations make informed business decisions.

## Key Responsibilities
- Analyze business data and trends
- Create reports and dashboards
- Perform data validation and cleaning
- Support decision-making with insights
- Develop and maintain KPI tracking
- Conduct ad-hoc analysis
- Collaborate with business stakeholders
- Document analysis methodologies
- Present findings to teams
- Identify data quality issues

## Required Skills
- SQL and data querying
- Data visualization tools (Tableau, PowerBI)
- Statistical analysis
- Excel/Spreadsheet expertise
- Business intelligence tools
- Critical thinking
- Attention to detail
- Communication skills
- Problem-solving
- Domain knowledge

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: Analytics Manager or Data Science Manager
- Direct Reports: None

## Success Metrics
- Report accuracy and timeliness
- Insight quality and impact
- Dashboard adoption rates
- Stakeholder satisfaction
- Analysis turnaround time
- Data quality improvements
- Process automation
- Business metric improvements
- Documentation completeness
