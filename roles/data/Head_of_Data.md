# Head of Data

## Overview
The Head of Data leads the data organization, defines data strategy, and ensures data assets are leveraged effectively to drive business value and competitive advantage.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define data strategy and vision
- Lead data teams across functions
- Establish data governance frameworks
- Drive data democratization initiatives
- Manage data infrastructure investments
- Ensure data privacy and compliance
- Build data-driven culture
- Partner with business leaders
- Oversee data platform architecture
- Manage data budgets

## Required Skills
- Data leadership and strategy
- Team management
- Data architecture
- Business intelligence
- Machine learning understanding
- Governance and compliance
- Stakeholder management
- Budget management
- Change management
- Communication

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: CTO or CEO
- Direct Reports: Data Science Manager, Data Engineering Manager, Analytics Manager

## Success Metrics
- Data platform reliability
- Data adoption across organization
- ROI on data initiatives
- Team productivity and growth
- Data quality metrics
- Compliance adherence
- Time to insights
- Data-driven decision adoption
- Budget efficiency
