# Content Marketing Manager

## Overview
The Content Marketing Manager develops and executes content strategy to attract, engage, and convert target audiences while building brand authority and thought leadership.

## Key Responsibilities
- Develop content strategy and calendar
- Create and edit various content types
- Manage content production workflow
- Optimize content for SEO
- Measure content performance
- Manage external writers and creators
- Ensure brand voice consistency
- Distribute content across channels
- Collaborate with subject matter experts
- Build thought leadership programs

## Required Skills
- Content strategy and planning
- Writing and editing
- SEO and keyword research
- Content management systems
- Analytics and metrics
- Project management
- Social media knowledge
- Visual content basics
- Brand storytelling
- Audience development

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: Marketing Director or CMO
- Direct Reports: Content Writers, Content Specialists

## Success Metrics
- Content engagement rates
- Organic traffic growth
- Lead generation from content
- Content conversion rates
- SEO rankings
- Social shares and reach
- Content ROI
- Publishing consistency
- Audience growth
