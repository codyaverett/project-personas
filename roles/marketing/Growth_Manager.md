# Growth Manager

## Overview
The Growth Manager drives user acquisition, activation, retention, and revenue through data-driven experiments, optimization, and cross-functional initiatives.

## Key Responsibilities
- Design and run growth experiments
- Optimize conversion funnels
- Analyze user behavior and metrics
- Drive user acquisition strategies
- Improve activation and onboarding
- Increase retention and engagement
- Collaborate with product and engineering
- Manage growth marketing channels
- Build referral and viral loops
- Report on growth metrics

## Required Skills
- Data analysis and A/B testing
- Growth hacking methodologies
- Marketing analytics tools
- Product analytics
- SQL and data querying
- User psychology
- Channel optimization
- Experimentation frameworks
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Strategic thinking

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: VP of Growth or CMO
- Direct Reports: Growth Marketing Specialists

## Success Metrics
- User acquisition rate
- Activation rate
- Retention curves
- Viral coefficient
- Customer acquisition cost
- Lifetime value
- Conversion rate optimization
- Experiment velocity
- Revenue per user
