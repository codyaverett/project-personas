# Product Marketing Manager

## Overview
The Product Marketing Manager bridges product and go-to-market, developing positioning, messaging, and launch strategies to drive product adoption and market success.

## Key Responsibilities
- Develop product positioning and messaging
- Create go-to-market strategies
- Lead product launches
- Conduct competitive analysis
- Create sales enablement materials
- Develop customer personas
- Produce product content and collateral
- Gather and analyze customer feedback
- Train sales teams on products
- Measure product marketing effectiveness

## Required Skills
- Product marketing expertise
- Market research and analysis
- Content creation
- Storytelling and messaging
- Launch planning
- Sales enablement
- Competitive intelligence
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Project management
- Analytical thinking

## Reporting Structure
- Reports to: VP of Marketing or Director of Product Marketing
- Direct Reports: None

## Success Metrics
- Product adoption rates
- Launch success metrics
- Sales enablement effectiveness
- Win rate improvement
- Message resonance
- Content engagement
- Sales team readiness
- Competitive win rate
- Feature adoption
